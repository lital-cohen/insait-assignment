Part 2 - How I Work with AI

The initiative: Swag Kit & Branded Greetings.

I chose this initiative because it's visual, tangible, and touches every employee. Here's how I actually worked through it - across three AI tools, with a lot of iteration.

Claude

My main AI partner.

Claude was my primary collaborator throughout this entire assignment. I used it to develop ideas, think through priorities, refine my writing, and build this presentation from scratch - including all the HTML. Every conversation pushed the work forward.

ChatGPT

Design iteration partner.

ChatGPT helped me bring the visual side to life - swag mockups, logo placement, layouts, and wording. It required many rounds of feedback and correction to get things right, but the iterative conversation format worked well for design work.

Canva AI

Design exploration.

I explored Canva AI during the design phase, but its suggestions weren't fully aligned with my vision. I used it more as a source of inspiration than a production tool - a useful starting point that helped me clarify what I was actually looking for.

AI is a powerful tool that speeds up the process and opens up possibilities - but the vision, the critical thinking, the creativity, and every final decision always come from the person using it. These tools helped me move faster and think more clearly. The work is still mine.
